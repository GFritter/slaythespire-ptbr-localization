# Localized Words/Terms

As you might know, some terms, expressions and even simple words may suffer adaptations to the target language. Due to cultural characteristcs or to avoid ambiguity.

The following tables shows the Words/Expressions adaptations to fill better in the Brazilian cultural scenario. If you dont agree with any localized term or have a better suggestion, please let us know.
